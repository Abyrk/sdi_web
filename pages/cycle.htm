About this page

This uses the google maps api, google charts and JavaScript. I defined a series of points to give the cycle route around the Isle and added a marker with info window at the start of the route. I also added a scalebar to the map.

The JavaScript works out the elevation at each point and uses it to create the chart. It also calculates the total distance of the route (using google's geometry library) and that is used in the title on the horizontal axis.

Further developments could be to add a scale to the horizontal axis of the chart and to link a point on the route with the elevation at that point.
